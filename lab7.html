Me and why my music taste is awesome

My name is Kennedy Craig and my music taste is really awesome. To prove it to you, I'll share some stats from my spotify wrapped of 2025. To start I listened to 311 different genres last year. I feel like someone who listens to so many different types of music can't be wrong frankly. My top song of 2025 was Animal I have Become by Three Days Grace, which I actually saw live like 2 weeks ago, so you know I'm devoted to my music. I've made it my New Years Resolution to burn all of my spotify playlists onto CDs so that I have something to show for the Spotify family plan supscription that I don't pay for. I've also been collecting albums when I find cool ones at the store, or I'll order my favorites online. I also have some albums that I bought through Itunes or Amazon Music and then burned onto a CD because they weren't available officially. I will take recordings, but most of the time I like to buy albums to support my favorite artists :D.

Me and CDs

Why?

I started my CD collection earlier this year. I've been collecting vinyls for a few years, but CDs are cheaper and more accesible, and because of the lower price I feel more comfortable buying albums from artists I know I like but haven't heard a lot from yet, which has helped me expand my music taste. I bought my boombox for 14 dollars on ebay with money I got from Christmas, and it's pretty awesome. It has FM radio which I do use andddd a cassette player which I will likely never use...but it's cool.

How I Pick Albums/CDs to Buy

Most of my CDs I get from scrounging around in the bookstore. I like to go straight to the CD section when I'm in a Barnes and Noble or Schuler's. Most of the time I'll skim through all the CDs and stop when I see an artist I like or an artist I have interest in and want to hear more of. I don't actually find a lot of artists albums I love in bookstores, but I love shopping for CDs in bookstores because I find really cool compilations and albums I never would have listened to otherwise. With my absolute favorite albums, if I can't find them in store I'll buy them online. I found out that when you buy a CD on Amazon you also get the MP3 files for all the songs, so that is a definite plus when I'm buying my favs.
